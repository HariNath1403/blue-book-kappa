    Generic Name

    Abacavir Sulphate 300mg tablet

    Brand Name

    Ziagen

    Prescriber Category

    A*

    Indication(s)

    Antiretroviral combination therapy of HIV infection in adults and adolescents from 12 years of age.

    Dosage

    Adult:300mg twice daily or 600mg dailyChildren:i. Weighing 14 to 20kg: one-half of a scored abacavir tablet twice dailyii. Weighing ?20kg to 25kg: one-half of a scored abacavir tablet in the morning and one whole tablet in the eveningiii. Weighing at least 25kg: according to adult dose

    Prescribing Restrictions

    i. Patients unsuitable or failed other HAART treatmentii. Patients who have renal impairment (CrCl < 50ml/min) when Abacavir and Lamivudine fixed-dose combination is not recommended

    Use in Pregnancy

    This drug should be used during pregnancy only if the benefit outweighs the risk to the fetus.

    Source: Drugs.com—pregnancy—abacavir

    Use while Breastfeeding

    Abacavir appears in breastmilk in small quantities. Very little information is available on the safety of its use during breastfeeding.

    Source: Drugs.com—breastfeeding—abacavir
